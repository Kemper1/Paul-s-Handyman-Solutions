My Story

Before starting this business I worked as an Electrical Engineer in various capacities for over 40 years — including working for NASA and the US Navy. The experiences and skills I've gained as an engineer, as a DIY homeowner, and as a woodworking teacher have given me a unique skill set to troubleshoot, diagnose, repair, and frequently improve just about anything.

I've been happily married for over 36 years, and we've raised 6 wonderful children (who have also learned to be very "handy"!).

In addition to running this handyman business, I've also helped hundreds of people across Lexington, KY and the US in digitizing and organizing their family videos, photos, and slides through PandaPix.

Basically, I'm just an all-around handy guy that would love to help you with your home improvements and repairs.
